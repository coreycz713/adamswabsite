Who is Nailed It Renovations?

Nailed It Renovations is a contracting and subcontracting business. It was founded by Adam Kinnaman roughly four years ago with a clear purpose: to build a dependable business that allowed flexibility for family life while delivering quality workmanship to the local community.

Adam has spent much of his life working odd jobs after school and throughout his career, steadily building practical, hands-on experience across a wide range of projects. Over time, those skills and work ethic helped him establish a loyal and consistent clientele.

As the business grew, so did its reputation for reliability, adaptability, and honest craftsmanship. Today, Nailed It Renovations serves the Kitsap, Washington area with a focus on quality renovations, strong client relationships, and a balanced approach that supports both professional standards and personal life.
